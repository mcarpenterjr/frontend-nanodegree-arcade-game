Unicorn Death Run

A JavaScript Game

Now with even MORE CANVAS!

The Story...

Charwee, a simple Unicorn from the planet UniCornaCopia, has wandered off and found himself here on earth in the Artic Circle. Using the arrow or WASD keys, navigate Charwee across the water to the grass where Poppi the King of UniCornaCopia is waiting to teleport you back to the land filled with Enchanted and Mystical Horses.

BEWARE... Your journey across the water will be filled with attempts by derranged narwahls to prevent Charwee from returning to UniCornaCopia, and keep him for their own. Or silence his existance, how ever you want to look at it...
